Problem: If you see empty space (gray area) around your logo in the circular masks, your favicon has too much padding.

Goal: The logo should fill the entire circle/square with minimal or no visible gray background.

‚úÖ How to Fix

  1. Open your logo in an image editor (Photoshop, Figma, Canva, etc.)
  2. Remove all transparent padding around the logo
  3. Export as PNG with the logo touching all edges of the canvas
  4. Use a favicon generator (like RealFaviconGenerator.net)
  5. Replace the generated favicons in the images/ folder
